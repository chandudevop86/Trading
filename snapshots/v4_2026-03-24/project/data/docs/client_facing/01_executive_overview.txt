TRADING APP EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

Overview
This Trading App is a unified trading workspace designed for market analysis, strategy execution, trade review, and reporting.
It brings live market data, multiple technical strategies, contract mapping, execution flow, and reporting into one operational platform.

Business Value
The application helps traders and operations teams reduce manual work by combining market monitoring, strategy testing, signal generation, contract preparation, and reporting in one place.

Core Capabilities
Live OHLCV market data viewing.
Multiple strategy engines.
Options contract selection.
Futures contract mode.
Paper execution workflow.
Live-execution-ready architecture.
Telegram alerting.
CSV, HTML, and PDF reporting.

User Benefits
Faster review of opportunities.
Structured trading workflow.
Centralized trade logs.
Repeatable backtesting and reporting.
Clear separation between analysis and execution.

Target Usage
This platform is suitable for active traders, internal trading desks, research teams, and semi-automated strategy operations.
